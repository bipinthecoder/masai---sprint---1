Cafe Latte
Latte is a coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk. The word comes from the Italian caffè e latte[3] [kafˌfɛ e ˈlatte], caffelatte [kaffeˈlatte] or caffellatte [kaffelˈlatte], which means "coffee & milk". The word is also sometimes spelled latté or lattè in English with different kinds of accent marks, which can be a hyperforeignism or a deliberate attempt to indicate that the word is not pronounced according to the rules of English orthography.
